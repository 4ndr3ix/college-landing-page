Admission into MUSTIBRA COLLEGE

Admission into Mustibra College of Information Technology And Management Studies, Kano, shall be open to candidates with the requisite qualifications. This is subject to written and/or oral examination as the College may determine. Admission into the College shall be open to all, irrespective of religion, ethnic group, gender, creed, and disability. All applicants for admission into Mustibra College must have attained 16 years of age.

Entry through the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME)

The admission requirements into the Diploma programmes of Mustibra College shall be as follows:

  1. All admissions into the College shall be through the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB).
  2. For admission to 100 Level (via UTME), candidates must obtain five (5) credits at SSCE (or equivalent) in relevant subjects at not more than 2 sittings, including credit passes in English and Mathematics; and attain acceptable points in UTME in relevant subjects.
  3. Those who meet the requirements for admission shall be subjected to a screening interview conducted by the College.
  4. The College shall not accept transfer students until after at least the first two years of its existence. Prospective students must also satisfy the faculty and departmental requirements.
Entry through JAMB Direct Entry (DE)

The requirements for admission by direct entry (200 Level) are as follows:

  1. For admission by direct entry (200 Level), candidates shall, in addition to having five (5) SSCE credits, obtain at least two (2) A’ level (or its equivalent) passes in relevant subjects, or possess ND with credit passes, or possess a good first degree in another field.
  2. Those who meet the requirements for admission shall be subjected to a screening interview conducted by the College.
  3. The College shall not accept transfer students until after at least the first two years of its existence. Prospective students must satisfy the faculty and departmental requirements.
